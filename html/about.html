About

Play as a General Utility And Repair Drone in this fast pace, team based, tug of war. Where team work is essential to get your pilot and yourself of the hostile planet that you crashlanded on. Collect energy cells to power up the ships engine while protecting both the pilot and the ship from hordes of hostile aliens. The twist is that their is another team of Guard's that also want to escape the planet. Good luck, and may the best team survive.

Collect the energy cells or die trying

The energy cells gives the game an exciting and competative element. The planet holds only one cell at the time, until that one is collected an stored in one of the ships it is an all out tug of war for that specific cell. Hope your up to the fast paced challange.
