News

Beta-time, are you ready?

26 September, 2015

We are really happy to announce to you that we will release the beta-version of Guard in November. We will let you guys duke it out under a whole weekend and collect as much feedback as possible to make the game as great as it can get. We will put out more information when it starts to get closer to beta-time in November. Hopefully you guys will look forward to this as much as we do. Until next time, have a nice day!

Next up: Gamescon

6 July, 2015

We have been incredibly lucky and gotten the chance to have our own booth at Gamescon 2015 in Germany. This is a major milestone for our small studio and we hope that we can meet some of you guys there. We will have some computers hooked up so that it will be possible to test our game in the state that it is at the time. All of us at G303 Studios are really looking forward to the event and will work even harder to get a good feel on the game while we are there. See you guys in Germany 5-9 of August!
